Our Policy

The 4th CD Green Party (GPSP) is guided by the four co-equal pillars of the international Green Party:
Grassroots Democracy, Ecological Wisdom, Social and Economic Justice
GPSP does not endorse candidates who accept corporate or PAC money.

 




For a Cleaner
Environment

Saint Paul and the 4th CD deserve a greener healthier environment: clean water, access to urban agriculture, curbside composting, and bikeable streets.




For a Fairer
Society

The Green Party envisions a Saint Paul/4th CD which celebrates diversity and encourages appreciation and cooperation between groups.




For a More Just
Economy

The Green Party of the 4th CD believes in livable wages and locally controlled economies that serve communities, not take advantage of them.
